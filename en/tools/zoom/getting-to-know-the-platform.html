Getting Started - Joining a Meeting

  • You can download the Zoom app from our Zoom's Download Centre. Otherwise, you can access it from your browser.
  • Each meeting has a unique meeting ID that is required to join.
  • If you are joining by telephone, you will need the teleconferencing number provided in the invite.

Zoom features

Zoom Meetings syncs with your calendar system and delivers streamlined enterprise-grade video conferencing from desktop and mobile.

Bring HD video and audio to your meetings with support for up to 1,000 video participants and 49 videos on screen.

Record your meetings locally or to the cloud, with searchable transcripts. The host and attendee can screen share by clicking the Share Screen icon.

Keep participants engaged: enable polling; Q & A; virtual hand-raising; etc.

From Meetings to Webinars

  • Host online events with up to 100 interactive video participants. Plans range from 100 to 10,000 view-only attendees.
  • Expand your impact with on-demand or recurring webinars with recording and auto-generated transcripts.
  • Mute / unmute panelists, and promote attendee to panelist, giving them audio and video capabilities for enahnced engagement.

Tips for Videoconferencing

  • Use video to promote face to face interaction, but always include an audio line as back-up.
  • Remind your team to mute when not speaking to avoid unneccessary distractions.
  • If chat is enabled, be sure to have someone monitoring it.
