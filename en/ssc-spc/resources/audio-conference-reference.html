GTS Audio Conferencing Service

Quick Reference Guide

Conference Invitations

Your participants need to know:

  • Date & time of the conference
  • Dial in phone numbers - include both the local 613 and tool-free numbers
  • Conference ID (7-digit access code)

Beginning a Conference

As the Moderator:

  1. Call either the local or toll-free number associated with your account
  2. Select your language
  3. Enter your Conference ID followed by the # (pound key)
  4. Press * (star key), then enter your Moderator PIN
  5. Press 1 to start or join your conference, or press 2 to change account options (like disabling Entry & Exit tones)

Top Star Touch Features

Available to Moderator
  • ## - Mute all lines
  • 99 - Unmute all lines
Available to Everyone
  • *6 - Mute your line
  • *7 - Unmute your line
  • *0 - Operator Assistance

Fun Fact: When all lines are muted by the Moderator, participants can still speak by pressing *7 to unmute!

Conferencing Tips during COVID-19

  • Use a landline phone, instead of your cellular phone
  • Start meetings on quarter hours, instead of on the hour or half-hour
  • When possible, schedule meeting outside of peak hours;
    • Before 09:00
    • 12:00 to 13:00
    • after 16:00
  • Use the local 613 number to connect whenever possible
  • If unable to connect via the local number, try the toll-free number

Update account information or add a Webex account with the GTS Change request from: http://www.gocconference.com/accounts/?lang=en
