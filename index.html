How it started

As an ambassador of GCtools (including GCpedia), I see the potential in using digital communications. At the same time, we're all busy with our day-to-day responsibilities. By giving you the ability to further enhance your GCpedia with GCbootstrap, you are tapping in to various designs and templates created by other Bootstrap users (back to 2011) and not having to reinvent the wheel!

If you can think of a common digital design, component or layout, it's probably already been created. GCbootstrap gives you quicker access to these existing resources and, hopefully, makes your life a little easier.

GCpedicure

GCbootstrap wouldn't have been possible if it weren't for the efforts of Chris from Global Affairs Canada's InnoLab and GCpedicure. Thank you!

The methodologies of using a GCpedia page with Bootstrap started with GCpedicure and it's what inspired me to extend this functionality. GCbootstrap simply extends GCpedia, GCpedicure by:

  • Utilizing the latest Bootstrap build (currently v4.1.3)
  • Restoring the basic functionality of GCpedia:
    • Content AND User Interface Language Flipper
    • Read, Edit source, View history, Favorites, Unwatch
    • Link to GCpedia profile, Talk, Preferences, My favorites, Watchlist, Contributions, Logging in and out
  • Creating an improved responsive design for various resolutions and devices
